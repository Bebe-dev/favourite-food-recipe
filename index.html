My favourite food is Spaghetti. Spaghetti is a type of pasta originating from Italy. It is made from flour, water and sometimes oil. Spaghetti national day is celebrated on January 4th yearly. There are different ways of preparing spaghetti but I will be sharing my way with you all.

Time of preparation: 30-35 mins
Ingredients Measurements
Spaghetti 1 golden penny pasta
Tomato 1 can tomato sauce
Chicken or any protein of choice chicken breast or thigh
Vegetable oil 2 tablespoon
Green Pepper 1 large green pepper
Red pepper 1 large red pepper
Yellow pepper 1 large yellow pepper
Red chilli powder 1 tablespoon
Onions 1 large onions
Garlic 3 cloves
Carrot 1 carrot
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Seasoning 2-4 cubes/sachets

    Procedures


  1. Add your spaghetti into boiling water. Also add salt to taste.
  2. Check occassionally if spaghetti is done while you continue the other steps. When cooked properly, remove from fire and sieve.
  3. Cut your chicken into strips, season with salt and pepper and saute in hot frying oil.
  4. Remove the sauted chicken from the pot/pan.
  5. Add chopped onions and minced garlic into the same frying oil and let it saute for 2 to 3 minutes.
  6. Add your chopped vegetable(red pepper, green pepper, yellow pepper, carrots) to saute lightly.
  7. Add your tomato sauce and a little amount of the spaghetti water.
  8. Add seasoning to your taste.
  9. Cover to let simmer for about 5 minutes.
  10. Add your spaghetti and your chicken into the pot/pan.
  11. Stir properly and serve.
  12. Enjoy alone or with a chilled drink.
